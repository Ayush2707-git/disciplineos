# DisciplineOS

DisciplineOS is an AI-powered Personal Operating System designed to help people align their daily actions with their long-term goals.

Unlike traditional habit trackers, DisciplineOS focuses on behavioral intelligence, identity growth, and future trajectory analysis.

## Core Philosophy

Most productivity apps track what you did.

DisciplineOS helps you understand:

- Who you are becoming
- Whether your actions match your goals
- What's slowing your progress
- The hidden cost of inconsistency
- What action will create the biggest improvement

## Key Features

### Mission Control
Daily high-impact actions based on your goals.

### Future Self Simulator
Visualize how current behavior affects future outcomes.

### Reality Gap Analysis
Compare who you want to become with what your actions actually prove.

### Discipline Debt
Track the accumulated cost of missed commitments and inconsistency.

### Personal Operating Manual
Discover your peak focus hours, productivity patterns, recovery methods, and behavioral triggers.

### Weekly Life Audit
AI-generated reviews that identify bottlenecks, wasted time, and growth opportunities.

### Goal Intelligence
Transform long-term ambitions into measurable daily execution.

## Vision

To become the operating system for personal growth, helping people make better decisions by understanding the relationship between today's actions and tomorrow's results.
